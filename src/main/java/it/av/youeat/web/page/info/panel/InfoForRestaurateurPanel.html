Are you the owner of a restaurant?

On YouEat you can advertise your restaurant!

Keep updated the "Restaurateur Blackboard": Every restaurant has a "Restaurateur Blackboard" where you can put the strong points of your restaurant, promotions, initiatives.

Improve the description: On YouEat everyone can modify the description of a restaurant, you can do it as well. Don't forget to add correct "tag" for your restaurant, tags are very important for the search.

Add featured pictures: Improve the visibility of your restaurant adding featured pictures.

YouEat is non-profit: Remember that YouEat is a non-profit project, free and aopen. YouEat is based on the work of a community of volunteer.

Do you want access to the Restaurateur Blackboard?

You must have an account on YouEat.
When you have an account, you must login on YouEat and send a friend request to the Administrator Alessandro Vincelli, use the "Friend" panel on the menù.
Then send a message to Alessandro where you say that you are the owner of a restaurant.

If you want more information, please write to: a.vincelli@gmail.com
